This is the collector of the information from SQL connection and reformats it to JSON. It will print out in organized print. 

Please note:
the two queries ran are show variables and show status. It will combine JSON format of both

to use this, replace ENTER_SERVER, ENTER_USER, ENTER_PASSWORD and ENTER_NAME, with your own SQL information

replace SQL_VARIABLE_NAME and SWL_VARIABLE_VALUE with your two columns in sql
